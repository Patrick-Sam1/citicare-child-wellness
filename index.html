About Citicare

Your Partner in
Child & Adolescent Wellness

Citicare Child & Adolescent Wellness is dedicated to supporting the mental and emotional well-being of children and adolescents. We provide compassionate, evidence-based care including assessment, counselling, psychotherapy, and family support tailored to each child’s needs.

Our focus is helping children feel safe, understood, and supported as they learn to manage emotions, build resilience, and grow in confidence. We are committed to closing the gap in mental health care by ensuring every child has access to quality, personalised psychological support.
