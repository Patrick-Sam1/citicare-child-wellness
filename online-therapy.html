Online & Virtual Therapy

Expert therapy from the comfort of your home or office. Flexible, accessible, and effective.

Virtual Care

Therapy, Anywhere
You Are

Our Online/Virtual Therapy is designed to make it as easy as possible to get the help and support you need right in the comfort of your home or office.

Our therapists come from a wide array of different backgrounds, practices, beliefs, and cultures. You can get everything from online therapy, CBT to DBT therapy with Citicare.

Citicare Online/Virtual Therapy offers affordable therapy as an alternative to in-office/physical therapy that allows a therapist to communicate with you remotely through text-based chat, phone calls, or video calls.
