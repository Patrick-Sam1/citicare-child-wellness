FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Things you need to know about psychotherapy and counselling services.

Why should I see a therapist?

People see a therapist for a wide range of reasons including stress management to a specific diagnosis. Sometimes an event occurs that causes an individual to seek out help, while for others, they may attend therapy because it provides a trained objective person to help them work through issues.

How long does therapy take?

There is no specific time frame when working on personal development and growth. Depending on the type of changes that you are looking to make and the variety of issues that you are attempting to work on, therapy can last from a few sessions to much longer.

What are the benefits of therapy?

Benefits include: gaining a better understanding of oneself, figuring out life goals, improving personal relationships, developing coping skills, enhancing problem-solving abilities, making positive changes to problem behaviors, and boosting self-esteem.

Is counselling confidential?

Yes, your counsellor provides a safe environment in which to explore your issues. We take your privacy very seriously and everything shared remains confidential within legal and ethical guidelines.
