Our Story

A Golden Touch in
Psychotherapy

Citicare Child & Adolescent Mental Wellness was established to promote the emotional, psychological, and social well-being of children and adolescents in Nigeria. We recognized the need for specialized mental health support that helps young people navigate challenges and achieve healthy development.

Our multidisciplinary team provides comprehensive, evidence-based services, including mental health assessments, counselling, psychotherapy, and family support. We are dedicated to creating a safe and nurturing environment where children and adolescents can build resilience, develop positive coping skills, and thrive.

With compassion, professionalism, and excellence, we empower young people and their families to achieve lasting mental wellness.

Our Values

The Principles That
Define Us

These core values guide every interaction and decision we make as a practice.

Professionalism

We apply best practice principles and conduct ourselves in a professional and ethical manner to deliver services that benefit our clients and community.

Strong Relationship

We value strong relationships as the primary vehicle for helping people achieve mental health and learn new ways of thinking, feeling, and behaving.

Competency

Our team possesses the skills, knowledge, and experience to provide effective care. We work within our areas of competence and continuously seek to grow.

Confidentiality

We understand the importance of a safe space. Your privacy is taken very seriously, and we ensure that everything shared remains confidential.
